> the number needs to have a sign with it (==signed==)

\-  ==negative==

> [!NOTE]
> Each signed variant can store numbers from −(2n − 1) to 2n − 1 − 1 inclusive
> where _n_ is the number of bits that variant uses

## Example ([[Signed]])

> [!NOTE]
> An `i8` can store numbers from −(27) to 27 − 1, which equals −128 to 127.
